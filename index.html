About the Club

Motto: Strength in Unity

We cultivate a culture of excellence through structured training, competitive exposure, and inclusive community programs. Our athletes compete across inter-college and district tournaments while also contributing to campus wellness initiatives.

Vision

To become a benchmark college sports program known for athlete development, integrity, and winning spirit.

Mission

Provide world‑class coaching, facilities, and competitive opportunities that elevate every student athlete.

Faculty Advisor

Dr. A. Sharma, M.P.Ed., Ph.D.
